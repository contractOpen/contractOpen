# Manifesto

I do not want to build dead demos.

I want to build products that can be used, tested, judged, criticized, improved, and eventually trusted.

I care about:

- software that actually ships
- systems that can grow into networks
- products that earn the right to become more ambitious

I am especially interested in the line between:

- centralized and community-hosted
- game logic and asset logic
- experimentation and durability

The goal is not to sound futuristic.

The goal is to make something real enough that the future has to respond to it.
